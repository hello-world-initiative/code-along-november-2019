For those who swim against the current

A different kind of vacation

At Travel Salmon, we believe a vacation can be more than just a trip. We curate travel packages for people who prefer their meals cooked over a campfire, who will take a sleeping bag over a king mattress, and who never forget to pack bug spray. There's nothing wrong with ordering dinner at a white tablecloth restaurant after a day on the beach - but why limit yourself? Nature is our suite, the Earth is our resort, and the best room service is what you catch in the local stream and bring back to your tent. If you'd rather count the stars in the sky than the ones next to the name of your hotel, let us help you create a vacation you'll never forget.
